Pandemic Legacy Season 1

Ann received the Pandemic Legacy Season 1 boardgame as a 2015 Christmas gift. Thus over the course of 2016 a committed group of 5 regular boardgamers gathered to play through the game, finally completing it in December. This is a record of their campaign to save the world - written as it happened. The reports include many spoilers.
