The End

Tags: General

The end is near. No not the end of this blog. The end of my time in London. In a couple of days I will be on a plane to South-East Asia. Most of the luggage has already left. I’m in that weird disconnected in-between state where I’m not sure what I should be doing. In any case, this blog may be neglected for the next couple of weeks as I complete the move and discover first-hand how good Internet access is in Malaysia. I have heard it is very good and hope to be back soon. Until then, goodbye.
